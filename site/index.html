About the project

Not just a login page. A full ecosystem with rules.

United Europe Crypto connects a public educational website with a private app. The public website introduces the idea, risks and structure. The app is where users register, receive their platform ID, submit deposit or withdrawal requests, complete lessons, manage profile data and follow reward progress.

The logic is simple: a disciplined community can learn faster, compare market conditions better and act with more structure than isolated users. The platform does not promise guaranteed income. It gives users a controlled environment where every important financial action is reviewed and recorded.

Earning logic

A clear model for activity, rewards and withdrawals.

Users can receive internal rewards through lessons, satellite structure, trading streaks and balance milestones. Additional trading positions are opened through active satellites: invited users who made a confirmed deposit from $500.

Withdrawal requests are available from $100 and only from earned funds. Deposited funds are separated from earned internal balance, which keeps the model easier to audit and explain.

Questions and answers

Typical questions before joining.

Is this website separate from the app?

Yes. This website is a public project presentation. The app is separate and handles login, registration, learning, assets, rewards, trading and admin-reviewed requests.

Does the project guarantee profit?

No. Crypto markets are volatile and involve risk. The platform provides structure, education and internal accounting, but it cannot remove market risk or guarantee income.

How do users receive access to learning?

Learning opens after a confirmed deposit from $500. The app shows available lessons, tests and reward status.

Can withdrawals go to another wallet?

Yes. The withdrawal wallet can be different from the wallet attached to the account, as long as the address is valid and the request follows the platform rules.

What are satellites?

Satellites are users invited by another user. They become active after a confirmed deposit from $500 and can unlock additional trading positions for the inviter.

Why are deposit and withdrawal requests reviewed by an admin?

Admin review keeps balance changes controlled, traceable and connected to each user. It also creates a clear signal history inside the admin panel.
